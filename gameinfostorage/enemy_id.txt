Interpretation of file:
Double exclamation mark (!!) marks the ID of enemy, e.g. !!00
Enemy object information is found on same line, in format:
{surf}/{name}/{attack}/{defence}/{max_health}/{weapon}/{movement_pattern}/{xp_yield}/{gold_yield}

!!G1 goblin/Goblin/10/10/50/foo/direct/50/10
!!O1 orc/Orc/30/5/25/foo/direct/50/10
!!VP violin_player/Violin Player/5/20/60/foo/still/30/20
